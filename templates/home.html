Welcome !

In conjunction with the Penfield Library Mission Statement, Archives and Special Collections contributes to enhancing the intellectual growth, creativity, and well-being of our community by creating opportunities for the public to engage with our historical collections. In particular, crowdsourcing transcriptions for the Millard Fillmore Papers collection provides the public with opportunities to read, understand, and contribute searchable text alongside the original written words with the outcome of a more open learning and research environment for all.
